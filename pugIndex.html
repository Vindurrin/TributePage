Timeline

  • 1624: Manhattan was "discovered" by Dutch settlers.
  • 1664: It was taken over by the English.
  • 1776: The American Revolution.
  • 1789-1790: New York is the capital of the USA.
  • 1898: New York merges all the cities under one banner.
  • 1898: Large waves of immigration through Ellis Island begin.
  • 2001: New York City is attacked by terrorist group Al-Qaeda and the Twin Towers fall.
